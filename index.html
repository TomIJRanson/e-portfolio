MSc Cyber Security Student

I used to be an Additive Manufacturing Research Technician. I specialised in industry grade 3D printing and commerical high density laser scanning.

Personal Goals

Professional Development

Personal Development

Technology and security are things I have a very real passion for. I want to take the skills learnt here and apply them to my personal hobbies. 

Earning Power
